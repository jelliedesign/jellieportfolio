Tiger Cub Childcare Concept

Tiger Cub Childcare was created as a personal project with the goal to appeal to the demographic of Asian Canadian children and their parents.

Logo

Tiger Cub Childcare gets it’s name from the Chinese representation of Tigers being “Bravery” and “Strength”. The logo is designed with the idea that the children will take on these qualities as their time in our presence comes to an end.

Favicons

Designed with the idea of legibility at very small sizes on different platforms

Letterhead

The letterhead is encorporates a cute and fun aesthetic while maintaining a level of maturity if needed.

Business Card

Simple and cute, displaying vital information in a clean fashion.

Envelope

Cute, fun and exciting for both parents and children to receive in their mail.

Storefront

Designed to be inviting for children and generally pleasurablel to look at. Windows are tinted to protect every child's privacy.
