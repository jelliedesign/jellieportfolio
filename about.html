About Me

Designer & Creative Professional

Hello! I'm a passionate designer specializing in creating meaningful and intuitive digital experiences. With a focus on Asian Canadian aesthetics, I bring a unique cultural perspective to my work.


My journey in design began with a fascination for how visual elements can communicate complex ideas and evoke emotions. Today, I combine strategic thinking with creative execution to deliver projects that not only look beautiful but also solve real problems.


When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new art exhibitions, experimenting with photography, or enjoying bubble tea at my favorite local spots.
